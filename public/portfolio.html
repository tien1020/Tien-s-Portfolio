Sallie Shoppe

Javascript - MERN - Authenticate

Sallie Shoppe is an e-commerce website that allows users to sell their products and buy products from others. It brings more options to people who want to shop with adorable prices and earn money from selling their unused stuff. Users need to sign-up and enter first name, last name, email, and password to create an account. When they log in, they can pick a product they would like to buy and then place an order. Besides, users can upload their products for selling with title, price, and description. Users can edit their profiles and products' information also.
